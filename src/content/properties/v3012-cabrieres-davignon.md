On the edge of the village of Cabrières-d'Avignon, this property blends seamlessly into its surroundings, combining traditional architecture with amazing views of the Luberon.

The ground floor comprises: spacious living area with great volumes, bathed in natural light, featuring an open-plan kitchen that flows into the dining room and sitting room; additional sitting room, enhanced by oak flooring, adding warmth and character to the space; utility room; loo; large garage.

On the garden level: bedroom, enjoying direct access to the outside; large and lovely wine cellar.

The first floor accomodates: two elegant bedrooms sharing a beautifully renovated shower room with a walk-in shower and gorgeous Zellige tiling; master bedroom with an en suite shower room; separate loo.

The garden features a covered terrace, providing an ideal space for summer dining. A path lined with terraced walls leads to a shaded area, perfect for the warmer days. The chlorine pool, measuring 8m x 4m, fits perfectly into this beautiful setting.

Amenities include: underfloor heating; double glazing; automatic watering system; electric gates; mains water; mains drainage; fibre optic internet.

Thanks to its prime location, stunning views, spacious interiors,  character and private garden, this property offers a peaceful living environment or excellent potential for rental investment, all in the heart of Provence.
