Facing the cliffs of Saint-Pierre and on the edge of the village of Venasque, this ultra-modern stone property offers a true haven of peace and relaxation, enhanced by panoramic south-west views, expansive terraces with a heated swimming pool, and a beautifully landscaped Japanese-inspired garden. The house is accessed via a charming footbridge leading to a partially covered wooden terrace, ideal for hosting or relaxing in the shade, with the pool and a centennial olive tree as a striking focal point.

Set on a landscaped plot of 3,794 m², the property offers approximately 200 m² of living space arranged over three levels, thoughtfully designed to maximize light, views, and privacy.

The ground floor features: fully equipped kitchen; adjoining pantry; sitting room opening onto balcony that captures uninterrupted views over the surrounding countryside; WC. Large sliding bay windows allow natural light to flood the interior and frame the scenery throughout the day.

The upper floor includes: principal bedroom suite accessed via striking iron and glass staircase with built-in wardrobe and private balcony overlooking the cliffs of Saint-Pierre; spacious bathroom fitted with double sinks and generous walk-in shower. 

The garden level comprises: large bedroom suite with direct access to private terrace whose bathroom includes bathtub, walk-in shower and double sinks; two additional bedrooms with en suite bathrooms and shared access to separate terrace; laundry room; utility room; extensive linen storage room.

The exterior spaces are carefully maintained and designed with a subtle Japanese influence, enhanced by clean lines, terraces at varying levels, and mature oak trees. The 9m x 4.5m heated swimming pool integrates perfectly into the setting, offering both privacy and open views.

Amenities include: double-glazed windows; reversible air-conditioning throughout; two hot water heaters; automatic irrigation; mains water; Starlink internet; electric gate.

Located in the countryside of Venasque, yet within a 15-minute walk of the village, this contemporary stone home combines architectural elegance, modern amenities, and a rare natural setting in one of Provence’s most beautiful villages. Its elevated position, thoughtful layout, and seamless connection between interior and exterior spaces make it an ideal primary residence or refined second home.
