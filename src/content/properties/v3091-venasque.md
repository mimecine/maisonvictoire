Situated on the edge of the desirable village of Venasque, just a short stroll from restaurants, shops, and a bakery, this exceptional property combines authentic Provençal character with modern comforts and timeless elegance. With spacious interiors, lovingly preserved historical details, and a large private garden, it offers both charm and convenience in one of the region's most sought-after settings.

The ground floor comprises: welcoming garden room with exposed beams, working fireplace, and stone floors revealing the original rock foundation; sitting room; dining room; fully-equipped kitchen, cleverly converted from the original wine cellar, complete with a stone countertop cut from a single slab.

The first floor houses: grand salon with ornate wood fireplace, original crown molding, and terracotta tile flooring throughout; three comfortable light-filled bedrooms with impeccable period wallpaper; bathroom with original tiles; separate WC. A spiral stone staircase with wrought iron railing is an architectural highlight of this level.

The enclosed garden is planted with native trees and flowers and includes: generous dining terrace shaded by a pergola; greenhouse/potting shed; swimming pool with complete privacy and open views. The land across the road is part of the property, ensuring protection from any vis-à-vis.

Amenities include: fibre internet; mains water and drainage; oil-fired central heating with radiators; two water heaters; electric gate; window screens; electric shutters in garden room and kitchen; new roof; private parking for several cars.

This property stands out for its authenticity, proportionate spaces, and sun-filled rooms, making it as welcoming as it is elegant. A rare opportunity to own a true Provençal home, perfect as a primary residence, holiday retreat, or rental investment in one of the Luberon’s most picturesque villages.
