Nestled at the foot of the Luberon, this magnificent 18th-century magnanerie offers an exceptional living space of 600 m², harmoniously combining the charm of the past with modern comfort. Located on the edge of a village, it guarantees perfect tranquility without being isolated. A majestic cypress-lined driveway leads to the property, announcing the unique character of this exceptional place.

Main Residence  
This property exudes remarkable authenticity with its French ceilings, exposed wooden beams, and period stonework, giving each room a unique character.

Ground Floor: A large entrance hall, a vast living/dining room of 80 m² featuring a monumental fireplace, a bright kitchen opening onto a terrace for outdoor dining, a utility room, a laundry room, a gym/games room, and a wine cellar.

South Wing: A spacious 50 m² independent suite with an en-suite bathroom, benefiting from direct access to the gardens and inner courtyard.

West Wing: An entrance hall, two storage rooms, an orangery, and a charming attic space comprising a bedroom and an en-suite bathroom, ideal for children with its reduced ceiling height.

First Floor: A sumptuous 65 m² master suite with an adjoining bathroom/shower and dressing room, as well as three additional bedrooms and two shower rooms.

Outdoors  
The Mediterranean-style landscaped gardens offer an enchanting setting with mature trees, dry stone walls, and a charming inner courtyard. The 11x6 m swimming pool, framed by authentic stone walls, has recently been renovated with a high-quality liner and features a salt treatment system and automatic pH control for easy management. Shaded terraces invite relaxation or outdoor dining.

Amenities and Features  
- Two covered carports  
- A storage area for garden tools  
- Two wells and an authentic stone fountain  
- Fiber optic, automated gate, outdoor lighting with motion detectors

Comfort & Technical Aspects  
- Hybrid heating with thermal exchanger & gas boiler  
- Reversible air conditioning in the master suite  
- Ceiling fans in three bedrooms  
- Four fireplaces, including a large Castellas fireplace with heat recovery  
- Double glazing in main rooms  

Location  
This exceptional property is just a few minutes’ walk from the village, offering easy access to local amenities while benefiting from a peaceful and privileged setting. Combining Provençal charm, elegance, and modern comfort, this rare home is a unique opportunity in the heart of the Luberon.
