Located in the heart of the Luberon valley, this striking Provençal Bastide is surrounded by rolling hills and picturesque woodland yet lies within walking distance of all local amenities. The property enjoys bright living areas, landscaped gardens, superb views of the surrounding valley, and a wonderfully tranquil setting.

Upon entry you are greeted by a grand foyer with double-height ceilings and a traditional staircase adorned with wrought-iron balustrades – complete with a guest WC. The ground floor enjoys panoramic views throughout and comprises: open-plan dining and sitting room with a charming stone fireplace; bright, fully equipped kitchen which opens onto a breakfast terrace for morning sun and boasts an oversized picture window overlooking the valley below; cosy second sitting room with access to a covered terrace; luxurious ground-floor master suite with a contemporary shower room and private living area.

The first floor is composed of: spacious double bedroom suite with adjoining office/sitting area and private bathroom; three further generous double bedrooms, sharing a modern shower room; large linen cupboard already plumbed for an additional bathroom if desired. 

Below, the garden level offers additional accommodation for guests, extended family, or rental income. This floor comprises: lovely office space; living room with kitchen; double bedroom (currently used as a gym) with adjoining shower room; workshop; technical room housing the gas-fired boiler; direct access to the double garage.

The bastide is set within beautifully landscaped gardens, designed for both privacy and year-round enjoyment. Mature trees and established hedges provide utter privacy, while a 12m x 6m swimming pool, complete with an electric safety cover, is set above the bastide, surrounded by a large terrace. A spacious pool house and covered terrace, equipped with a summer kitchen and pizza oven, offers the perfect setting for outdoor dining and entertaining. The pool house is fitted with roller shutters. From the pool area, you can admire both the bastide’s charming façade and the panoramic views across the Luberon valley. The grounds also feature a mature olive grove (producing approximately 25L of olive oil), majestic century-old oak and cedar trees, and a lovely variety of flowering plants, including lavender and oleanders.

Amenities include: gas-fired central heating; mains water; private water source (well); fibre-optic internet; double garage with electric doors; electric gates; automatic watering system; alarm and security cameras.
This exceptional bastide offers the perfect blend of Provençal charm, modern comfort, and breathtaking natural surroundings. Whether as a primary residence, a bucolic holiday retreat, or a lucrative Bed & Breakfast business, the property promises a relaxed, refined lifestyle in one of Provence’s most sought-after settings.

With its spacious interiors, superb outdoor living spaces, and panoramic views, this home is ready to welcome its next chapter.
