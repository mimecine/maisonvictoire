Just a stone’s throw from the village of Saint-Saturnin-lès-Apt, this beautifully located property enjoys a peaceful and picturesque setting. Tastefully designed throughout, it seamlessly blends authentic Provençal character with modern comforts, offering a gorgeous and thoughtfully curated living spaces.  

The first floor comprises: bright sitting room and dining room with high ceilings, opening onto an exquisite terrace and bathed in natural light; spacious, elegant Fabre kitchen, and breakfast table that flows through to the dining area; beautiful bedroom - with gorgeous hardwood oak flooring and double aspect windows - with its private bathroom, WC and charming balcony; additional bedroom with bathroom and WC en suite.
Every room has been carefully considered, with meticulous attention to detail, creating harmonious and magnificent interiors. 

The garden level is composed of: bedroom with adjoining bathroom and WC with tomette flooring; lovely double bedroom - currently used as an office; laundry room.
This level is enhanced by exposed wooden beams and terracotta floor tiles, adding warmth and character to the spaces.  

Outside, the landscaped gardens offer a natural, immaculately kept setting: olive trees, oaks, vines and Mediterranean plants make up a peaceful environment. A superb terrace runs alongside the house, revealing a magnificent view of the Luberon valley, the Mourre Nègre and the village church - with stone accent walls and fabulous views, it offers an incredible space for dining and entertaining. The property also enjoys a 12m x 4m heated swimming pool, as well as a lovely shaded terrace adorned with stone arches, from which you can also admire the view.

Amenities include: air conditioning in the upstairs bedrooms; electric heating; double glazing; water softener; automatic watering system; mains water; electric gates.

With its great sense of privacy, perfectly landscaped gardens, meticulously designed interiors and prime location on the edge of the lively village of Saint-Saturnin-lès-Apt – with the renowned Le Saint-Hubert restaurant and local shops – this property offers a superb setting for everyday living.

It is a place where comfort, style and authenticity come together effortlessly.
