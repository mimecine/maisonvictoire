Ideally located in a peaceful location in the very heart of Châteaurenard town centre, this fully renovated town house offers exceptional volumes, a spacious garage and multiple possibilities for use, combining comfortable living with strong rental potential.

The accommodation comprises, on the ground floor: large 50 m²  garage; independent single-storey studio apartment (67 m²) composed of living room, bedroom, bathroom, WC; direct access to a private enclosed garden.

The first floor is composed of: spacious 60 m²  living room flooded with natural light thanks to large picture windows, with the possibility to install the kitchen of your choice; separate pantry; principal suite with bedroom, bathroom, separate WC; second bedroom which may be used as a dressing room.

The second floor comprises: two additional bedrooms; bathroom; separate WC; versatile room suitable for a TV lounge, library, office or gym; insulated attic providing useful storage space.

Outdoor spaces include a private enclosed garden of 50 m²  on the ground floor as well as a generous south-facing terrace of 36 m²  on the first floor with a reinforced structure allowing the installation of a jacuzzi or small plunge pool.

Amenities include: double glazing; reversible air-conditioning; fibre-optic internet possible; mains water; mains drainage; new roof; new electrical and plumbing installations; 50 sqm garage (rare for a town centre).

A rare opportunity in Châteaurenard, offering generous living space, excellent flexibility and an easy town-centre lifestyle within walking distance of shops and amenities.
