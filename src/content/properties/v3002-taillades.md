This impressive property offers an open-concept floor plan with spacious and airy volumes, and stunningly landscaped, utterly private grounds. The garden accommodates a luxuriously large swimming pool and a beautiful pond shaded by a mature willow tree. Offering a solid foundation, and full of character, this house requires refreshing and modernisation to create a truly exceptional, personalised home. 

The accommodation comprises, on the ground floor: large entrance hall; open plan kitchen/dining room with large island unit; formal dining room; sitting room arranged around the fireplace; TV room; office or bedroom; entrance hall for rear door leading out to the covered terrace; utility room; guest loo. The ground floor also provides additional, and independently accessed, accommodation comprising two double bedrooms, a kitchenette, and a bathroom with loo. The ground floor is air-conditioned.

The first floor is composed of: master suite with large walk-in wardrobe and a shower room with loo; additional suite with adjoining shower room and loo benefitting from access to a private balcony overlooking the property’s pond; double bedroom; guest bathroom with shower; guest loo.

The second floor comprises: large double bedroom; child’s bedroom; guest loo; attic storage.

The property also benefits from a 2-car garage fitted an electric door and inspection pit.

Outdoor living space is provided by a large west facing covered terrace with a decorative stone pillars. The property also enjoys terraces to the south and east sides, with decorative balustrades, for enjoying the sun at different points of the day. The unusually verdant garden is entirely hedged, granting complete privacy throughout. The property benefits from a private well that feeds the garden.

Amenities include: reversible A/C; electric radiators; double glazing; mains, Canal de Provence and borehole water source; septic tank conforming to regulations; fibre optic internet; electric gate and garage; automatic watering system. 

The property sits on the western edge of the Luberon with views of the famous mountain and an intimate but not isolated spot, close to all amenities. Beautiful cycling and walking paths lie on the property’s doorstep. This light and elegant property is welcoming for families and children.
