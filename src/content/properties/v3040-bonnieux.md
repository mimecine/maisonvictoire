This elegant property is located in the heart of the Bonnieux valley, overlooking the picturesque villages of Bonnieux and Lacoste. It is the ideal home for couples who are seeking a hazard free pied-à-terre in the Luberon. The property is surrounded by one hectare of land offering an idyllic setting with olive trees, lavender, a vegetable garden and vineyards. 

The property comprises off : a generous living area with terrace doors to maximize the views of the valley. The open plan kitchen with high-tech appliances has direct access to the garden. The spacious bedroom with ample cupboard space is serene and offers views of Lacoste. 
A modern bathroom with walk-in shower, a large sink, washing machine and toilet is generous and chic. 

The second room is currently used as a dressing, but could also be an office or a guest room with space for a double bed. 

The garden with its many terraces’ bathes in sunlight throughout the day and has picture perfect views from every corner. A large 8x4 pool enhances the holiday joys, as does the barbeque area located under the old pine trees. Part of the terrain consists of vineyards, which are maintained by a local wine maker. You will obviously get your fair share of the wine. 

Amenities include : A/C throughout the property ; thermodynamic water heater ; double glazing ; mosquito screens ; fibre internet ; large storage space ; ample parking space and solar panels on the roof ensure neglectable energy bills. 

The property was built in 2021 with a 10 years warranty for all construction and amenities and is very easy to maintain. The smart layout, high (cathedral) ceilings, large windows and efficient use of square meters provide a generous sense of space throughout the property.

This chic, yet practical property is the perfect holiday spot for those who want to enjoy a carefree Luberon lifestyle. It has all the sought-after villages of the area nearby and embodies the true Provencal dream. With ample chambres d’hôtes in proximity for your guests, you can keep this little gem just for yourself.
