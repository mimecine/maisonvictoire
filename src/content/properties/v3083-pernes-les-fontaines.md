This exceptional manor house is situated just a short walk from the charming and historic town of Pernes-les-Fontaines. This stately residence, a perfect blend of historical charm and modern comfort, offers an elegant lifestyle in the heart of the Vaucluse countryside.

Dating back to the 1700s, this manor house was meticulously renovated from top to bottom. Spanning 478 m², the home features six bedrooms and four bathrooms, thoughtfully laid out over two floors and a magnificent tower. Upon entering, you're greeted by a grand hall with a striking walnut door. The main living spaces are defined by a classic Provençal aesthetic, with outstanding attention to detail, marble floors, and authentic fireplaces throughout the property. A "papillon" staircase made from pierre d'Estaillades adds a stunning architectural feature. The kitchen is fitted with elegant pear wood cabinetry and polished granite countertops.

The main staircase leads to the master suite, which boasts an opulent bathroom finished in onyx stone and a private office. This level also features two further bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom. A second-floor bedroom and two additional rooms are accessed via a staircase that continues up to the iconic tower. The first floor also includes a spacious room above the garage with a chimney conduit and fabulous exposed oak beams. This vast room, with its high ceilings and enormous potential, would be perfect for a library or independent studio.

Modern comforts include an automatic watering system, an air-conditioned wine cellar, oil-fired heating, fiber internet, double glazing, an alarm system with cameras, and a video intercom.

The estate is set within 16 hectares of beautifully maintained grounds, offering a private and tranquil setting. The gardens are a true highlight, with 48 olive trees and a private source of water, in addition to city water and a connection to the Canal de Provence. The grounds are secured by an electric fence and feature an automatic irrigation system with a drip system for the gardens and olive trees. For parking, the property offers a garage and a separate carport with space for up to five cars.

This property would suit someone looking for an opulent Provençal estate.
