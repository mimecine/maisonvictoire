Ideally located at the gateway to both Mont Ventoux and the Luberon, this spacious modern property enjoys easy access to the medieval town of Pernes-les-Fontaines, within walking distances of its renowned restaurants, shops, and wide range of amenities. Offering all modern comforts and sleek contemporary finishes, the property also benefits from a gorgeous garden with exotic plantings and traditional pine woodland, and a terrific variety of outdoor living areas. The accommodation comprises a spacious main house with an independent guest wing, together with a separate three-bedroom gîte – ensuring strong potential for seasonal rental income.

The main house opens onto a vast open-plan space combining sitting room, dining area, and sleek contemporary kitchen. Large picture windows overlook the garden, and wide terraces adorned with a pergola draped in jasmine provide a wonderful setting for outdoor dining. Four spacious bedroom suites, arranged on either side of the living space, each with a private terrace and adjoining bathroom, offer an excellent level of comfort and style.

The west wing of the property, accessible via the outdoor walkway, faces the garden and the 12m x 4.5m swimming pool. It comprises a covered terrace with a fantastic living area for dining and relaxing in the shade, a kitchen and bar, as well as two adjoining suites with particularly generous volumes.

Located to the rear of the main house, the property's gîte benefits from a separate gated entrance and complete independence for guests or staff. On the ground floor, the gîte comprises: private terrace; entrance hall; living space composed of a sitting room and dining room; and a kitchen. A bedroom with ensuite bathroom and separate WC completes this floor. The first floor comprises: a large terrace; two additional bedrooms; and a bathroom with WC.

The carefully landscaped garden blends local pine trees with large exotic wooden planters, jasmine, pomegranate, and olive trees, offering a varied outdoor setting with poolside areas, lawn, and pétanque court.

Amenities include: reversible air conditioning; two electric gates; double glazing; mains and canal water supply; automatic irrigation system; electric vehicle charging point; parking; Starlink internet; laundry room; and storage areas.

This exceptional property harmoniously combines comfort and functionality in a stunning natural setting, with most of the accommodation arranged on a single level. Its layout with several independent spaces, numerous bedrooms with private terraces, and extensive facilities – from the pool house to the summer kitchen – make it ideal for seasonal rentals, large families, or any buyer seeking generous living space.
