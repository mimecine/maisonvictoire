Located in the heart of Ménerbes, one of the most desirable villages in the Luberon, this fully renovated house combines authentic character with modern comfort and offers a mix of contemporary finishes and natural materials. Set at the top of the village, the property enjoys spectacular panoramic views and a peaceful setting, all while being just steps from a great selection of restaurants, cafés, shops, and local vineyards. A historic village house, entirely remodeled to offer a luxurious standard of modern living.

The ground floor comprises: characterful vaulted entrance hall; two beautiful caves carved into the rock, offering a unique atmosphere - currently used as a sitting room and a game room; luxurious hammam; lovely library; loo; utility room; technical room.

The first floor is composed of: principal suite with a private bathroom and terrace; two bedrooms, each with its own en suite shower room and charming private terrace; loo.

The second floor accommodates: bright kitchen with a fireplace, opening onto a dining room, a spacious living room, and a TV room – creating a stunning ensemble, extended by a vast panoramic terrace; loo.

The third floor comprises two elegant bedrooms and an office, bathed in light thanks to large windows opening onto the view, beneath a beautiful high ceiling.

Outside, several terraces offer exceptional views over the Luberon, the Monts de Vaucluse, Mont Ventoux, the citadel of Ménerbes, and the Cévennes. 
This space is ideal for al fresco dining thanks to easy access to the kitchen.

Below, the 5m x 2m swimming pool – a rare asset in the heart of the village – blends seamlessly into the landscape, highlighted by porcelain stoneware tiling. 

Amenities include: electric heating and underfloor heating on the ground floor; double glazing; mains drainage; mains water; fibre optic internet; electric shutter for the pool.

Perfectly located, the house offers several bedrooms with independent access, creating a beautiful ensemble ideal for hosting guests. With its high-quality renovations, great volumes, prime location, and breathtaking views, it provides fabulous living, in the centre of the village of Ménerbes.
