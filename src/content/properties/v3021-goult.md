Just a short walk from the ever-desirable village of Goult, this elegant contemporary villa offers the perfect blend of modern comfort and serene countryside living. Boasting outstanding Luberon views, a private garden, swimming pool, and multiple terraces, this property is the ideal retreat.

The accommodation comprises, on the ground floor: open plan kitchen and sitting room flooded with light and fitted with a cozy pellet-burning stove; master bedroom with adjoining shower room and WC. The kitchen benefits from direct access to the dining terrace that enjoys jaw-dropping views of the surrounding Luberon countryside. The open plan living space is also air-conditioned. A practical laundry room and access to the garage complete the ground floor. 

The first floor comprises: large double bedroom with adjoining office space that could be converted into a bathroom; large double bedroom with the possibility to split to create two separate bedrooms; guest shower room; guest WC. 

The garden is thoughtfully designed, with two terraced areas, ideal for outdoor dining and relaxation. The 7m x 3.5m swimming pool is fully secured with a gate, ensuring peace of mind. Mature trees surround the property, enhancing privacy and creating a lush setting. A garage offers ample storage space, and the property is completely enclosed with an automatic gate for added security.

This beautiful property offers the best of both worlds - great privacy while being just a short walk from Goult's local restaurants, bars, cafés, boulangerie, butcher, and grocery store. Enjoy complete tranquility without sacrificing convenience. With its stylish décor, incredible location, and generous outdoor space, this villa is the perfect Luberon getaway, a comfortable family home, or a potential rental investment.
