The property offers unrivalled panoramic views stretching from the Alpilles mountain range to Mont Saint Victoire to the south, and across to the easternmost point of the Luberon valley. Likely the finest view the Luberon has to offer.

Vast motorised patio doors open fully to reveal the landscape beyond, framing panoramic views of the Alpilles, the Luberon valley and Mont Saint Victoire. This incredible living space opens directly onto a large dining and seating terrace, overlooking the valley below. Adjoining the kitchen, a utility room offers additional fitted storage, a wine fridge, and laundry appliances.
