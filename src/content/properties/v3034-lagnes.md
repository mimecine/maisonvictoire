Located in the heart of the charming village of Lagnes, just a stone’s throw from local shops and amenities, this property offers the perfect blend of authenticity and convenience in a highly sought-after setting. With natural light streaming throughout it, the house enjoys a bright, sun-filled atmosphere all day long.

The ground floor accommodates: an entrance hall lined with elegant glass French doors; a welcoming dining room; a fully renovated kitchen fitted with quality materials and high-end finishes; and a guest WC.

The first floor comprises: a cosy sitting room currently used as a TV lounge; a more spacious second sitting room featuring a beautiful fireplace and opening onto a balcony – ideal for enjoying the village atmosphere. This level also includes an independent studio with bedroom, bathroom, WC, kitchenette and dining area. The studio benefits from its own separate entrance, offering flexibility for guests or rental potential.

The second floor offers: two charming bedrooms sharing a bathroom with loo, along with a dressing room and a study, which could easily be converted into an additional bedroom, if desired.

Throughout, exposed beams and traditional Provençal ceilings highlight the character of the house.

Outside, a private inner courtyard provides a peaceful and intimate setting, ideal for outdoor dining.

Amenities include: oil-fired central heating; double glazing; fibre optic internet; mains water; mains drainage.The house also enjoys a cellar and a private parking space - a rare asset for a village house.

With its character, lovely renovations, and delightful outdoor space, this property offers comfortable and convenient living, in this sough-after Luberon village.
