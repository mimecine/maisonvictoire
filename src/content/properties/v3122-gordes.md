This contemporary stone property, located on the edge of the village and overlooking the valley, consists of a spacious main house, gîte, pool house, and swimming pool. Set on 1.38 acres of land planted with over 300 olive trees, it offers spectacular views of the Luberon, as well as the villages of Gordes, Roussillon, and Bonnieux, while enjoying a south-facing exposure.

The independent accommodation will appeal to future owners as an ideal setting for entertaining family and friends or seasonal rentals. Each part of the property has its own entrance and has been designed to take full advantage of the panoramic views. With the centre of Gordes just a 15-minute walk away, the property enjoys absolute privacy and tranquillity. It offers high-end amenities over approximately 330 m² of living space, including two garages that can accommodate up to five cars - a major asset for enthusiasts. 

The ground floor, adorned with Carrara marble flooring, boasts large sliding bay windows that bathe the living rooms in natural light. The house overlooks the Luberon valley, with stunning views through the wisteria draped pergola framed by a sea of olive trees. An elegant dining room and fully equipped open-plan kitchen create a convivial space, perfect for entertaining. An office, laundry room, and separate wing comprising a bedroom, large sitting room, en-suite bathroom, and kitchenette complete this level. The first floor is composed of two majestic suites with floor-to-ceiling picture windows, each fitted with large bathrooms equipped with underfloor heating.

The exterior is equally remarkable, with a 13m x 6.5m tiled Émaux de Briare swimming pool, from which the house and the village of Gordes stand out in the background - a picture-postcard view. Surrounded by a large terrace offering superb vistas, it is the perfect place to enjoy the sun or dine thanks to its fully equipped pool house. The pool house also offers a gîte with two bedrooms, bright sitting room with bay windows, open kitchen, and bathroom.

Features include: mains water; double glazing; sliding bay windows; reversible air conditioning; Acova oil-filled radiators; fireplace; central control of roller shutters on the ground floor; four water heaters; 13m x 6.5m saltwater swimming pool with electric cover; two garages, one with a mechanical pit; cellar and storage space; vegetable garden and greenhouse; outdoor lighting; automatic watering system; water softener; alarm system; fibre internet.

With its dominant position, panoramic views of the Luberon, easy access to the village of Gordes, complete privacy and high-end amenities, this property is a rare find on the market - an idyllic retreat in one of the most beautiful villages in France.
